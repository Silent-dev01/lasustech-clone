our certificate and programmes for 2023

OND

OND or ND is a Polytechnic award for a two-year course. This stands for Ordinary National Diploma,which is also a vacational undergraduate qualification that takes two years to to complete. it is awarded in field such as engineering, business and computing.
Holder of ND can be employed or be offered admission into Higher National Diploma (HND) and where through Direct entry admission, can be admitted to the 200 level course in a university.


HND

HND stands for Higher National Diploma, which is a vocational undergraduate qualification that takes two to three years to complete. it is awarded in field such as engineering, business and computing in Nigeria, HND holder can either proceed to postgraduate degree or continue with their professional careers. In between the ND and HND, there is always the need for a 1 or 2 year industrial training(IT). which is prerequisite for Higher National Diploma admission.


BSC

In full, BSC is a Bachelor of science. This term is used for a degree or university award. You earn BSC after completing a 4-year or 5-year course in any university. It is typically awarded in fields of study such as mathematics, natural sciences and social sciences.
Moreso, terms like B.Tech and BSC(Ed) are equivalent to BSC. They are also degree awards- just a difference in courses or discipline. While B.Tech is for graduates of engineering and technology-related courses, BSC(Ed) or B.Ed is for graduate of education courses.
